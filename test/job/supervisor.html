Job Posting for Production Supervisor at Swift

Requisition ID: 28266
Job Location(s): Providence, RI, US

Job Description

Exercises direct supervision over employees performing product assembly occupations primarily of a semi-skilled or skilled nature. Establishes a participative environment by being a role model, providing and facilitating communication, building a team atmosphere, and recognizing/fostering individual and team development. Builds and supports a customer mentality, aids in defining goals, identifies training and education needs, removes barriers, delegates responsibility/authority as appropriate, monitors progress of the team, encourages continuous improvements, and recognizes the performance of team members. Performance requires a knowledge of manufacturing techniques and systems. Applies appropriate methods, procedures, practices, and policies so as to achieve an economical and efficient operation. Provides leadership enabling area to demonstrate total quality in safety, quality, delivery and cost

Job Responsibilities

  • Performs supervisory duties relating to employees in assembly functions and designates working plans and assignments to provide efficient and effective production. Reassigns personnel where required in order to meet service commitments, schedule changes, etc
  • Organizes and prioritizes work assignments to complete assignments/tasks on time, with quality, and has the ability to anticipate problem areas in advance. Applies creative approaches and ideas to solve problems maximizing effective use of appropriate resources.
  • Builds and supports customer mentality. Responds with commitment and sensitivity toward satisfying the needs of internal and external customers. Ensures quality standards are met.
  • Evaluates situations objectively and demonstrates the ability to make decisions that are intended to have a positive impact on work performance of area and the total business. Helps to define appropriate goals.
  • Reports to production manager of any problems involving other departments which affect established procedures, schedules and standards. Provides input to production manager on improvements or changes which may benefit the overall operation. Performs special assignments or projects as the production manager may direct.
  • Provides performance management through coaching working with production operators and production team coordinators. Monitors employees’ progress. Takes necessary actions to build and foster continuous improvement philosophies with each team member.
  • Recommends changes in status and salary adjustments; approves absences and overtime, per alignment with production manager; administers necessary disciplinary action according to company policies.
  • Demonstrates knowledge of interpersonal and people leadership skills. Able to effectively handle complex people issues.


Background & Skills

  • Associate's degree or equivalent from two-year college or technical school
  • Basic understanding of Quality and Safety and Environmental Management Systems
  • Practical experience and knowledge of lean tools and problem solving
  • General understanding of manufacturing assembly principles
  • Basic computer skills
  • Communicate with all levels of the organization
